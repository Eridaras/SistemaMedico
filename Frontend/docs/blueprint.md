# **App Name**: MediTrack Pro

## Core Features:

- Patient Registration: Allow medical personnel to register new patients in the system with their basic information and medical history.
- Appointment Scheduling: Enable scheduling and management of patient appointments, including date, time, doctor, and reason for visit.
- Medical Records: Provide a secure and organized system for storing and accessing patient medical records, including diagnoses, medications, and allergies.
- Billing and Payments: Handle billing processes, including generating invoices, processing payments, and managing insurance claims.
- Reporting: Generate standard reports such as revenue or other operational statistics.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#A0D2EB) to evoke calmness and trust.
- Background color: Very light desaturated blue (#F0F8FF).
- Accent color: Light orange (#FFB347) to highlight important actions and notifications.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif for a blend of modernity and approachability.
- Use clean and recognizable icons for navigation and actions.
- A clean and organized layout to easily find patients or data.
